About App


Description

Automated Paper Evaluator for EFL is an automated paper tool dedicated to English as Foreign Language (EFL) users. The measures are contextualized for EFL's academic writing.


How this works?

This tool is dedicated mainly for EFL teachers. By submiting students academic paper, the tool will provide a score and brief feedback. Once the essay is inputted the result will be recorded on front page. The score and feedback are not final score, but come as a basis of further writing improvement.
